MACAO: First Workshop On Mathematics and Algorithms for Cryptographic Advanced Objects

25-28 November 2019, Wollongong.
Aim | Speakers | Program | Location | Contact | Participants | Photos
Aim of the workshop

    This workshop is supported by INRIA and University of Wollongong and is viewed as a collaborative project between France and Australia as well as between mathematicians and computer scientists.
Speakers

  • MACAO team
    • Jean-Claude BAJARD - On Polynomial Modular Number Systems over Z/pZ (Talk)
    • Antoine JOUX - Fully homomorphic encryption modulo Fermat numbers (Talk) and Isogeny based Cryptography
    • Fabrice ROUILLIER - The price of certifications when computing and working with roots of polynomial systems (Talk)
  • Invited speakers
    • Valérie BERTHÉ - Lattice reduction and continued fractions (Talk)
    • David HARVEY - Recent results on fast multiplication (Talk)
    • Allan STEEL - Algebraic Attacks with GPUs (Talk)
    • Marco TOMAMICHEL - Fault-tolerant quantum advantage with shallow circuits in 3D (Talk)
  • Short talks
    • Arinta AUZA - Lattice-based Distributed Randomness Beacon
    • Mohamadali MEHRABI - Improved Sum of Residues Modular Multiplication Algorithm (Article)
    • Youming QIAO - Tensors based Cryptography
Program

    The workshop will be held during the entirety of the four days between the 25th to the 28th of November.

    Each day will be organized as follows: in the morning, two speakers will give us a lecture on some of their works. In the afternoon, we will discuss and share ideas.
